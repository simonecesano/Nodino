This is stuff I created while trying to figure out how to do a node-based environment for SVG graphics - [clipboard.html](clipboard.html): experiment with pasting images - [flow.html](flow.html): an experiment on shapes driven by another shape (the size of the rectangles is determined by the Y-axis position on the bezier - [picture.html](picture.html): a picture being used as data input for vector output - [poisson.html](poisson.html): poisson distribution of points, and [rough.js](https://roughjs.com/) used to draw the lines - [svgtopdf.html](svgtopdf.html): converting SVG to pdf via [pdfkit.js](http://pdfkit.org/) and [svg-to-pdf](https://github.com/alafr/SVG-to-PDFKit)
